# Year 4 Strategy: The Gladiator

**Theme:** Agility, Speed, and Pressure
**Objective:** To maintain poise and precision under fire.

## Quarterly Focus

### Q1: Improvisation (Months 1-3)
*Goal:* "Yes, And".
- **Theory:** Keith Johnstone (Impro).
- **Drill:** Word Association games.

### Q2: The Debater (Months 4-6)
*Goal:* Rebuttal and Clashing.
- **Theory:** Competitive Debate formats (British Parliamentary).
- **Drill:** Speed drills (speaking 200wpm+ with clarity).

### Q3: Crisis Management (Months 7-9)
*Goal:* Handling hostility.
- **Theory:** Media Training manuals.
- **Drill:** The "Hot Seat" (Ask GF to interrupt/heckle you).

### Q4: The Synthesis (Months 10-12)
*Goal:* Controlled Chaos.
- **Theory:** Trial Advocacy.
- **Drill:** Extemporaneous speaking.
