# Year 1, Month 1: The Neutral Foundation

## Daily Schedule
*Standardized for Workdays (Mon-Fri)*

- **07:30 - 08:00 (Wake Up):**
  - **Hydrate.**
  - **Random Page Ritual (5 min):** Open book, read, transcribe, apply.
  - **Body Scan (10 min):** Lie on floor, tense and release every muscle group.
  
- **09:00 - 17:00 (Work - Stealth Drills):**
  - **The Check-in:** Set a timer for every hour. When it goes off, check your jaw and shoulders. Drop them.
  - **Typing Posture:** Feet flat on the floor. Spine aligned.

- **12:00 - 14:00 (Lunch):**
  - **12:00 - 13:00:** Eat & Socialize. Listen more than you talk.
  - **13:00 - 14:00 (Solo Dojo):** 
    - 20 min: Reading (Theory).
    - 40 min: "The Neutral Stand" practice. Stand against a wall, spine touching, and read a boring technical manual aloud without moving anything but your mouth.

- **17:00 - 18:00 (Dead Zone - Mon):** 
  - Podcast listening: "The Daily" or similar high-quality audio. Shadow the host's intonation.

- **18:00 - 20:00 (Practice Window):**
  - **Mon/Tue/Thu:** Social time (GF). Practice "Active Listening" - repeat back what she says in your own words before responding.
  - **Wed:** Deep work (if free).

- **Night:**
  - **Sleep by 23:00-00:00** to ensure 07:30 wake up is fresh.

## Weekends (Flex/Deep Practice)
- **Sat:** 2 Hours. Record yourself reading a news article. Watch it back without sound. Watch it back with sound/no video. Critique.
- **Sun:** Rest / Reading.
