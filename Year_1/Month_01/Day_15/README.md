# Day 15 Drill: The Breath Anchor

**Context:** The Dead Zone (Transitions between work/life).
**Duration:** 5-10 minutes.

**Exercise:**
1. Stop what you are doing.
2. Inhale through nose for 4 seconds.
3. Hold for 4 seconds.
4. Exhale through mouth for 4 seconds.
5. Hold empty for 4 seconds.
6. Repeat 10 times.

**Application:**
Use this immediately before your stand-up meeting or code review.
