# Year 1, Month 1: Syllabus

## Theme: Unlearning Bad Habits

### Reading List (Theory)
1. **Aristotle's Rhetoric** - Book 1, Chapters 1-3 (Definitions of Rhetoric).
   - *Goal:* Understand Ethos, Pathos, Logos definitions.
2. **An Actor Prepares** (Stanislavski) - Chapter 1: "The First Test".
   - *Goal:* Understand "stage fright" and muscular tension.
3. **The Debater's Guide** - Chapter 1: " The Value of Debate".

### Video/Audio Resources
- **TED Talk:** Amy Cuddy - "Your Body Language May Shape Who You Are" (Focus on the 'Power Posing' concept for physiology).
- **Audio:** "The Vocal Arts Podcast" - Episode on Breath Support.

### Deadlines
- **Week 1:** Aristotle Readings + Notes.
- **Week 2:** Stanislavski Chapter 1.
- **Week 3:** Debater's Guide + TED Talk.
- **Week 4:** Review and Synthesis.
