# Year 1 Strategy: The Instrument

**Theme:** De-structuring and Re-building
**Objective:** To strip away physical and vocal tensions, establish a neutral and responsive "instrument" (body/voice), and internalize the fundamental structures of persuasive argument.

## Quarterly Focus

### Q1: Neutrality & Awareness (Months 1-3)
*Goal: Identify and release tension. Understand your baseline.*
- **Acting:** Stanislavski’s "Circle of Attention", Relaxation, Neutral Mask concepts.
- **Rhetoric:** Aristotle's *Rhetoric* - The 3 Appeals (Ethos, Pathos, Logos).
- **Debate:** Propositional Logic, Assertion-Reasoning-Evidence (ARE).
- **Key Habit:** The Daily Body Scan & Breath Journaling.

### Q2: The Voice (Months 4-6)
*Goal: Power, range, and clarity without strain.*
- **Acting:** Linklater/Berry voice basics. Removing the "Software Engineer Slump".
- **Rhetoric:** The 5 Canons: Invention & Arrangement.
- **Debate:** Identifying Fallacies, Cross-Examination basics.
- **Key Habit:** Humming & Articulation drills in the shower/car.

### Q3: The Argument (Months 7-9)
*Goal: Structural thinking and public speaking confidence.*
- **Acting:** Uta Hagen’s Object Exercises (Privacy in Public).
- **Rhetoric:** Style (Low/Middle/High styles).
- **Debate:** Flowing a debate (Note-taking), Rebuttal structure (4-Step Refutation).
- **Key Habit:** Impromptu Speaking (Table Topics) - 1 minute per day.

### Q4: Connection (Months 10-12)
*Goal: Merging voice, body, and logic into performance.*
- **Acting:** Basic Monologue work. "Talking to" vs "Talking at".
- **Rhetoric:** Memory (Memory Palace basics).
- **Debate:** The 5-paragraph case construction.
- **Key Habit:** The "Boss Assignment" Live Recording.
