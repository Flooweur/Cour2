# Year 2 Strategy: The Character

**Theme:** Psychology, Truth, and The Other
**Objective:** To move beyond the self and inhabit the mindset, emotions, and logic of others.

## Quarterly Focus

### Q1: The Listening Machine (Months 1-3)
*Goal:* Radical active listening and Meisner technique.
- **Theory:** Sanford Meisner on Acting.
- **Drill:** The Repetition Game (adapted for solo/stealth).
- **Assignment:** The Verbatim Transcript (mimicking a recorded conversation exactly).

### Q2: The Logical Detective (Months 4-6)
*Goal:* Deconstructing arguments and uncovering subtext.
- **Theory:** Cialdini's "Influence".
- **Drill:** Identifying Cognitive Biases in code reviews.
- **Assignment:** The "Steel Man" Argument (Arguing the opposing view better than they can).

### Q3: Emotional Access (Months 7-9)
*Goal:* Safe access to emotional range.
- **Theory:** Uta Hagen - Emotional Memory (Sensory).
- **Drill:** Sensory recall (coffee smell, cold wind) during breaks.
- **Assignment:** The Private Moment (filmed).

### Q4: Character Composition (Months 10-12)
*Goal:* Building a role distinct from yourself.
- **Theory:** Laban Movement Analysis (Eight Efforts).
- **Drill:** Walking in character.
- **Assignment:** The Contrast Monologue (High status vs Low status).
