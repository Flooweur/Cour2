# Year 5 Strategy: The Master

**Theme:** Integration and Legacy
**Objective:** To transcend the techniques and simply "be".

## Quarterly Focus

### Q1: The Auteur (Months 1-3)
*Goal:* Creating original work.
- **Theory:** Directing/Playwriting.
- **Drill:** Writing a 10-minute keynote.

### Q2: The Mentor (Months 4-6)
*Goal:* Teaching others.
- **Theory:** Pedagogy.
- **Drill:** Explaining complex concepts simply (Feynman technique).

### Q3: The Legacy (Months 7-9)
*Goal:* Defining personal style.
- **Theory:** Autobiography of leaders.
- **Drill:** Personal Manifesto.

### Q4: Completion (Months 10-12)
*Goal:* The Final Project.
- **Theory:** None.
- **Drill:** The 1-Hour One-Man Show.
